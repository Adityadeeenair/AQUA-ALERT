How are locations added to the map?
When you submit a report, we store the coordinates and show a red marker on the Water Map.
Do I need an account to report?
No. You only provide basic contact information so we can follow up if needed.
What counts as a “Safe Water Source”?
Reports tagged as “Safe Source” are counted under Safe Water Sources on the dashboard.
Is my data shared?
Only aggregated, anonymized statistics are shown publicly on the dashboard.
